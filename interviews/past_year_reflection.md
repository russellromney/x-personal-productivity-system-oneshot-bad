# Past Year Reflection Interview

A guided self-interview for reflecting on the past 12 months. This is meant to be completed in one sitting, ideally during your annual review.

Read each question slowly. Write your honest answer. Don't edit yourself—first thoughts are often truest.

---

## Before You Begin

- Block 60-90 minutes of uninterrupted time
- Put your phone away
- Go somewhere you can think clearly
- Have your calendar from the past year open for reference
- Consider having your daily/weekly reviews available to jog memory
