# Identity and Values Interview

A guided self-interview for exploring who you are and what you stand for. This isn't about who you think you should be—it's about discovering who you actually are.

Complete this annually, or whenever you feel disconnected from yourself.

---

## Before You Begin

- Block 60-90 minutes of uninterrupted time
- This interview requires honesty, not performance
- Write your first thoughts—don't edit for how they sound
- If a question feels uncomfortable, that discomfort is information
